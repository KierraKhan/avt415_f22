Project 1 : Ideas

Moonbow

Moonbow is an online clothing store that sells Indian clothing. Traditional clothing (Sari, Lancha, Anarkali Suit, etc.) and jewelry. A rewards program and customer login would be on the fourth page.

Paracosm

Paracosm is a bookstore. Special offers would be on the home and/or inventory pages. For a forth page, it could be a rewards program, customer login, or sale offers.

Zephyr

Zephyr is a cafe that sells coffee. It would sell different coffee blends, sizes, and treats. It would also show different locations for a forth page idea. And maybe a rewards program as well.
