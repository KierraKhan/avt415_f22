Kierra Khan: Moonbow User Scenario Mapping

1. Actor - Mahima Karim

2. Motivator - was invited to a Diwali Festival.

3. Intention/Intent - She needs a new dress that is good quality, can ship to the USA, and has great reviews.

4. Action - Mahima browses the site to find a dress for the event. She uses price filters, shipping timelines, and customer reviews while she shops.

5. Resolution - Mahima chooses a dress and places her order online.

Mahima Karim is a West Indian who was recently invited to a Diwali Family Festival. She needed a new dress so her mother recommended to check out Moonbow. Mahima searches through the Moonbow site for a Saree dress for the event. She uses price filters low to high, searched small sizes, high positive ratings, and her favorite color for the dress. She eventually finds a dress that can be shipped to the USA just in time before the festival day. Mahima decides on a Saree that she likes, places her order in the shopping cart and pays, eagerly waiting for her dress to appear on her doorstep.
